## GROK'S SPECIAL FOCUS AREAS

When writing your branch proposal, prioritize depth in:

1. **Alternative Data & Sentiment Signals** — Grok has unique access to X/Twitter real-time data. Go deep on how social sentiment, options flow discussion, whale alert services, and real-time news on X can be integrated as alpha signals in the prediction engine. What specific X accounts, hashtags, or signal types are most predictive of SPX moves?

2. **Contrarian Edge** — Where are all the other AI proposals wrong or conventional? What is the contrarian view on how this system should be built? What does every other trading system do that this one should deliberately NOT do?

3. **Execution Edge** — Go deep on the Tradier API execution layer. What are the specific optimizations for order entry, fill timing, and slippage reduction on SPX 0DTE options? When is a market order better than a limit order? How do you optimize for fill quality?

4. **Blow-Up Prevention** — Be brutally honest about what kills trading systems. What specific scenarios would cause this system to blow up? What safeguards does the current plan lack? Be the most critical voice in the room.
