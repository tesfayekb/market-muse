## GPT'S SPECIAL FOCUS AREAS

When writing your branch proposal, prioritize depth in:

1. **Prediction Engine Architecture** — GPT has strong knowledge of ML model architectures. Go deep on what specific models, features, and training pipelines would give the best edge for SPX options prediction. Name papers, name libraries, give actual feature lists.

2. **Options Pricing & Greeks Mathematics** — GPT should bring mathematical rigor to the strategy selection and risk management pillars. Don't just say "monitor delta" — specify exact thresholds, formulas, and adjustment logic.

3. **Code-Backed Proposals** — Where possible, support your architectural proposals with Python pseudocode or actual implementation sketches. This makes your proposals immediately actionable.
